Road Safety Prediction Model
For a competition sponsored by SparkCognition, a global leader in computing analytics and artificial intelligence, teams were tasked with testing their new automated building platform, Darwin, and demonstrate its ability to study real world problems.

The problem: Nearly 1.25 million fatalities are caused by car accidents every year, making it the 8th leading cause of death in the world.

The approach: analyze a large collection of data on car accidents that occured over a year to identify which road and vehicle conditions are more likely to result in a fatality

The result: a finalist in the competition, leading to a presentation to the board of members at SparkCognition
