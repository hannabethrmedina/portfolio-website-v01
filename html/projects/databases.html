ANALYSIS OF SOCIAL INFLUENCES ON EDUCATIONAL FUNDING

The goal: construct a data warehouse to join previously siloed datasets in order to derive interesting insights from the combined data


The approach: analyze a National Household Travel Survey (NHTS) regulated by the U.S. Department of Transportation's Federal Highway Administration, containing data on households and household members, and join this with a Public Elementaryâ€“Secondary Education Finance Dataset collected and maintained from the United States Census Bureau on the same year in order to identify certain relationships between educational funding and social factors such as household income, race, and gender.

Utilizing Google Cloud Platform and a series of data science tools such as Postgres, BigQuery, Apache Beam and Dataflow, Jupyter Notebooks, and Data Studio, the datasets were combined by gradually enriching, cleansing, and transforming the data to create an overall unified view in order to ascertain information about the desired relationships.
